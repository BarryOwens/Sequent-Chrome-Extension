SEQUENT

Sequent allows you to quickly create a playlist using YouTube links on any webpage your browsing.

To Begin, download the Chrome Extension here.

Tell me More First

Extension Permissions

Extension permissions are required when you first download Sequent. This is so that Sequent can pick up any YouTube when you ask it to create a playlist. Sequent doesn't store any details so your data is safe.

Why Use Sequent

There is nothing worse than that feeling when a track ends and your left in waiting or searching for the next song to put on. Sequent allows you to have a playlist that will play through all links found on whatever page you select. Some examples could be a Facebook group, a music blog or Subreddit.
